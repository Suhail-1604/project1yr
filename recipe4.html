Chettinad Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish pieces (e.g., pomfret or kingfish)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1 tablespoon Chettinad masala powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the Chettinad masala powder and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  4. Stir in the coconut milk, tamarind pulp, and fish pieces. Simmer until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Serving Suggestions:

Chettinad Fish Curry pairs well with steamed rice or dosa.

Tips:

  • Adjust the spice level by adding more or less Chettinad masala powder.
  • You can use any firm-fleshed fish for this recipe.
  • If you prefer a thicker curry, reduce the amount of tamarind pulp.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro for extra freshness.
  • Feel free to add curry leaves for a traditional flavor.
