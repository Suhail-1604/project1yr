Masala Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/4 cup chana dal (bengal gram lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked rice and lentils separately into smooth pastes using enough water.
  3. Mix the rice and lentil batters together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8 hours until it doubles in volume.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  5. Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa and cook until it turns golden and crisp.
  6. Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it's cooked through.
  7. Remove the dosa from the pan and serve hot with coconut chutney and potato masala.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve Masala Dosa hot with coconut chutney and potato masala.

Tips:

  • For a crisp dosa, make sure the pan is hot before pouring the batter.
  • Add a little oil or ghee while cooking the dosa for extra flavor.
